📝 Simple Side Note — The Sidebar Notepad for Google AI Mode & English Learners

Tired of switching tabs every time you want to jot down a new word or phrase from Google AI Mode?

Simple Side Note lives in your browser sidebar — so you can take notes without ever leaving the page.

✨ Key Features:
• ⚡ Instant Sidebar Notes — Open with one click and start writing. Save your note anytime with a single button — or just press ⌘/Ctrl+S.
• 🖱️ Capture from Any Page — Select any text → Right-click → "Add to Memo". Perfect for saving AI-generated definitions and example sentences (with the source link, optional).
• 💾 Save & Organize — Give each note a title, then search (⌘/Ctrl+F), sort, and 📌 pin your favorites to the top. Build your personal vocabulary list or study journal with ease.
• 🧹 Auto-clean & Trash — Let old, unpinned notes tidy themselves after 7, 30, or 90 days (off by default). Deleted notes move to Trash and stay recoverable for 30 days, so nothing is lost by accident. Pin 📌 anything you want to keep forever.
• 🗄️ Backup & Restore — Export all your notes to a single file and import them back anytime. Switching computers or reinstalling? Your vocabulary list comes with you — nothing gets left behind.
• ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts — Save and search without touching the mouse. Shortcuts match your OS (⌘ on Mac, Ctrl on Windows) and can be turned off in Settings.
• 🖼️ Rich Notes — Paste images, adjust font size, and add color to highlight key points.
• 📥 Export Anywhere — Download any note as HTML or Markdown to review later in Word, Google Docs, Notion, or Obsidian.
• 🎨 3 Themes — Light, Dark, and Warm. Study comfortably day or night.
• 🖥️ Feels Native — Uses your operating system's own font (San Francisco on Mac, Segoe UI on Windows) so it blends right in.
• ⚙️ Your Way — A Settings tab lets you tailor how it works: keep unsaved drafts, toggle confirmations, enable shortcuts, and more.
• 🔒 100% Private — All notes stay on your device. Nothing is sent to any server — and with one-click Backup, your list is always safe in your own hands.

🎯 Perfect for:
• English learners using Google AI Mode to look up words and phrases
• Students who want to save AI explanations without losing their place
• Anyone who takes notes while browsing the web

💡 How to use with Google AI Mode:

Search a word or phrase in Google AI Mode (e.g. "levitating means")
Open Simple Side Note in the sidebar
Type or paste the definition, give it a title, and press 💾 Save (or ⌘/Ctrl+S)
Build your vocabulary list without ever switching tabs

Start studying smarter. Install Simple Side Note and keep your notes right beside you.
